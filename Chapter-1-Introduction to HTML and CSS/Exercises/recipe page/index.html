Vegetable Fried Rice

Vegetable Fried Rice is a quick and delicious dish made with rice, colorful vegetables, and simple seasonings. It's perfect for a fast lunch or dinner!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, corn, beans)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish

Method

  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and saut√© for a few seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and cook for 3-4 minutes until slightly tender.
  4. Add the cooked rice and toss everything together.
  5. Pour soy sauce over the rice and mix well.
  6. Season with black pepper and salt as needed.
  7. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly.
  8. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
