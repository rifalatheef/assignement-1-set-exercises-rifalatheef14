Breaking Bad

Breaking Bad is a crime drama television series that follows Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine producer. After being diagnosed with terminal cancer, he partners with a former student, Jesse Pinkman, to secure his family’s future by entering the drug trade.

Cast & Creative Team

  • Bryan Cranston as Walter White
  • Aaron Paul as Jesse Pinkman
  • Anna Gunn as Skyler White
  • Creator: Vince Gilligan

Characters

Walter White: A chemistry teacher who becomes a drug kingpin.

Jesse Pinkman: Walter’s former student and drug partner.

Skyler White: Walter’s wife who becomes suspicious of his actions.

Reviews

Widely regarded as one of the greatest television series of all time, Breaking Bad received critical acclaim for its writing, acting, and character development. It has a 9.5/10 rating on IMDb.
